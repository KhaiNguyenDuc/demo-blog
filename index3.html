International Traditional Food Fair 2023

From 7th September until 16th September 2023

Global BBQ Festival: A Journey of Grilled Delights

Embark on a flavorful journey of grilled delights at our Global BBQ Festival. Experience the diverse barbecue traditions from around the world as you savor tender ribs, smoky kebabs, flavorful pulled pork, and more. Immerse yourself in the aromas of grilling and enjoy live cooking demonstrations by renowned pitmasters. Discover unique marinades, sauces, and smoking techniques that define different barbecue cultures. Join us for a finger-licking festival celebrating the art of barbecue and the joy of outdoor cooking.

Chocoholic Paradise: A Decadent Chocolate Festival

Indulge your sweet tooth at our heavenly Chocolate Festival, a paradise for chocoholics. Immerse yourself in the world of chocolate, from artisanal cocoa bean selection to exquisite truffles and velvety hot chocolate. Discover the rich history and art of chocolate-making through interactive workshops, tastings, and demonstrations by renowned chocolatiers. Treat yourself to a variety of chocolate-infused desserts, confections, and beverages, and uncover the secrets of this beloved indulgence. Join us for a truly divine chocolate experience that will satisfy all your cocoa cravings.

Gourmet Burger Festival: Elevating the Classic Burger

Celebrate the ultimate comfort food at our Gourmet Burger Festival, where we take the classic burger to new heights. Sink your teeth into juicy, handcrafted burgers featuring premium ingredients and innovative flavor combinations. From towering cheeseburgers to vegetarian delights, experience a burger extravaganza that caters to all tastes. Indulge in artisanal buns, house-made sauces, and gourmet toppings, all expertly paired with craft beers and hand-spun milkshakes. Join us for a burger feast that will satisfy your cravings and redefine your burger expectations.

Street Food Fiesta: Exploring the Flavors of the World

Take a culinary journey through the vibrant streets of our Street Food Fiesta. Sample a variety of international street foods, from mouthwatering tacos and savory samosas to delectable dumplings and spicy noodles. Immerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere, enjoy live music and cultural performances, and discover the rich tapestry of global street food traditions. Satisfy your cravings for bold flavors, discover hidden gems of street cuisine, and experience the diverse culinary heritage of different cultures, all in one lively festival.

Seafood Spectacular: Dive into Fresh Ocean Delicacies

Immerse yourself in a seafood extravaganza at our Seafood Spectacular festival. Delight in a wide array of fresh catches from the ocean, including succulent prawns, tender scallops, and flavorful fish. Indulge in seafood paella, oysters, grilled lobster, and other tantalizing dishes prepared by top seafood chefs. Explore the flavors of coastal cuisines, learn about sustainable fishing practices, and enjoy live cooking demonstrations. Dive into the world of seafood and savor the ocean's bounty at this unforgettable festival.

Vegan Vibes: A Plant-Based Food Festival

Join us at our Vegan Vibes festival, a celebration of plant-based cuisine. Explore the world of vegan flavors, from innovative meat alternatives to vibrant vegetable dishes. Indulge in delicious plant-based burgers, mouthwatering dairy-free desserts, and nutritious smoothie bowls. Engage with experts in vegan cooking, attend workshops on sustainable living, and learn about the health benefits of a plant-based diet. Discover the creativity and versatility of vegan cuisine and be inspired to embrace a more compassionate and sustainable way of eating.

Farm-to-Table Festival: Celebrating Local Produce

Experience the freshness and flavors of locally sourced ingredients at our Farm-to-Table Festival. Celebrate the bounties of local farms and artisans as you enjoy farm-fresh produce, artisanal cheeses, and handcrafted goods. Discover the farm-to-fork journey, from seed to plate, through interactive exhibits and farm tours. Indulge in farm-inspired dishes prepared by talented chefs, and learn about sustainable farming practices. Immerse yourself in the authentic tastes of the region and support the local food community at this unique festival.

Spice Market Extravaganza: A Journey of Flavors

Embark on a sensory adventure at our Spice Market Extravaganza. Immerse yourself in the intoxicating aromas and vibrant colors of spices from around the world. Explore exotic spice blends, sample spicy delicacies, and learn about the history and cultural significance of spices. Discover the art of spice pairing and experiment with new flavor combinations. From fiery curries to aromatic teas, experience the diverse and captivating world of spices in this immersive festival that will awaken your taste buds and ignite your culinary imagination.
